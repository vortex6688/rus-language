Present Simple

Questions Pattern (шаблон вопроса): 
question word (вопросительное слово) + "Do/Does" Verb/Auxiliary Verb (глагол ("Do/Does")/вспомогательный глагол) + Noun (Местоимение)
Answer Pattern (шаблон ответа):
Noun (Местоимение) + Verb/Auxiliary Verb (глагол/вспомогательный глагол)

Auxiliary Verb:
I + Am , He/She + Is, You/They + Are

Regular Verb:
I/You/They + Do, He/She + Does

Examples (Примеры):

- Where are you from?

- I	am Russian. (Noun + am) (Я Русский)
- I	am from Russia. (Noun + am) (Я из Русский)

- Where is He/She from?

- He/She is	Finish. (Noun + is) (Он/Она Фин)
- He/She is from Finland. (Noun + is) (Он/Она из Финляндии)

- Where are They from?

- They are French. (Noun + are) (Ты Француз/Они Французы)
- They are from Frence. (Noun + are) (Ты из Франции/Они из Француии)


Noun + Regular Verb

- How(как) *do* you like your coffee?
- I like my coffee black. (Я люблю черный кофе без сахара), I like my coffee with cream/milk (я люблю кофе со сливками/молоком),
  I like my coffee sweet/I like sweet coffee (я люблю сладкий кофе)

- How(как) *does* He/She likes His/Her coffee?
- He/She like(s) His/Her coffee black.

- How(как) *do* they like their coffee?
- They like Their coffee black.

------------------------------------------------

- What *do* you do in your free time? 
(- What *do* you usually(обычно) do in your free time? )
(- What *do* you normally(обычно) do in your free time? )
- I study psychology in my free time. (In my free time, I study psychology.)

- What *does* He/She do in His/Her free time? (In His/Her free time, He/She studies psychology.)
- He/She Studies psychology in His/Her free time.

- What *do* They do in their free time?
- They study psychology in their free time. (In their free time, They study psychology.)
