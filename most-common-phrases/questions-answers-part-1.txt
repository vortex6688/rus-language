Questions:

1) What is your name? 
   Как тебя зовут?

2) How are you?
   Как дела?

3) Do you have any plans for the evening?
   Какие у тебя (у вас - formal/official/respectful way to ask, or to address more than one person) планы на вечер?

4) Could you tell me what time it is now? (What time is it)?
   Не подскажите который час?

5) Where are you from? (What country are you from?) / What is your origin? 
   Откуда ты (вы - formal/official/respectful way to ask, or to address more than one person)? / Откуда ты (вы) родом?
