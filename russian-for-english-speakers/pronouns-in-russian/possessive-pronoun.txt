Я - I / I am	  
Mой - My / Mine (When we talk about a masculine gender object)
Моя - My / Mine (When we talk about a feminine gender object)
Example:

1) When we talk about a masculine gender object:
- It's my pencil. 
- Это мой карандаш. (карандаш - pencil)

2) When we talk about a feminine gender object:
- It's my purse.
- Это моя сумка.


Ты - You / You Are
Твой - Your / Yours (When we talk about a masculine gender object)
Твоя - Your / Yours (When we talk about a feminine gender object)
Example:

1) When we talk about a masculine gender object:
- Here is your train ticket.
- Вот твой билет на поезд. (билет - ticket, поезд - train)

2) When we talk about a feminine gender object:
- Here is your pen.
- Вот твоя ручка. (ручка - pen, рука/ручка - hand)

Он - He / He is
Его - His / His
Example:
It's his car.
Это его машина. (машина - car/vehicle/machine)

Она - She / She is
Её - Her / Hers
Example:
It's her gun.
Это её пистолет. (пистолет - gun/pistol)

Оно - It / It Is
Его - It / It's
Example:
This is it's leaf. (talking about a tree)
Это его лист.

Мы - We / We Are
Наш - Our / Ours (When we talk about a masculine gender object)
Наша - Our / Ours (When we talk about a feminine gender object)
Example:

1) When we talk about a masculine gender object:
- It's our city.
- Это наш город. (город - city/town)

2) When we talk about a feminine gender object:
- It's our country.
- Это наша страна. (страна - country)

Вы - You / You Are (either addressing more than one person or have a formal use to address a single person )	
Ваш - Your / Yours (When we talk about a masculine gender object)
Ваша - Your / Yours (When we talk about a feminine gender object)
Example:

1) When we talk about a masculine gender object:
- Here is your wallet.
- Вот ваш бумажник. (бумажник - wallet)

2) When we talk about a feminine gender object:
- Here is your lamp.
- Вот ваша лампа. (лампа - lamp)

Они	- They / They Are
Их (eekh) - Their / Theirs
Example:
It's their problem.
Это их проблема. (проблема - problem)
